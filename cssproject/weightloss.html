Monday – Full Body Strength + Cardio

  • Warm-up (5–10 min):
    Jumping jacks, arm circles, bodyweight squats
  • 3 Rounds Circuit:
    • 15 squats
    • 10 push-ups
    • 20 walking lunges (10 each leg)
    • 15 dumbbell rows
    • 30 seconds mountain climbers
  • Finish: 10-minute jog or brisk walk

Tuesday – HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes light cardio
  • 4 Rounds (40s work, 20s rest):
    • Jump squats
    • Burpees
    • Plank to shoulder taps
    • High knees
    • Russian twists
  • Cool down: Stretch & deep breathing (5–10 min)

Wednesday – Upper Body + Core

  • Warm-up: Arm swings, shoulder rolls, jumping jacks
  • Strength Training (3 sets):
    • 12 push-ups
    • 15 shoulder presses
    • 12 bent-over rows
    • 20 bicycle crunches
    • 30s plank
  • Cardio finisher: 5-min skipping or jog

Thursday – Lower Body Blast + Cardio

  • Warm-up: Light jogging, dynamic stretches
  • 3 Rounds Circuit:
    • 15 jump squats
    • 20 glute bridges
    • 15 sumo squats
    • 20 calf raises
    • 30s fast feet
  • Optional: Incline walk or stair climbing (10 mins)

Friday – Cardio Endurance + Core

  • Warm-up: Dynamic stretching + 5-min brisk walk
  • Cardio (30–40 mins):
    • Jogging
    • Cycling
    • Dance/Zumba
  • Core Blast (2 rounds):
    • 20 crunches
    • 15 leg raises
    • 30s side plank each side
    • 20 seated knee tucks

Saturday – Active Recovery / Yoga / Stretch

  • Light activity (30–45 mins):
    • Yoga flow (sun salutations, warrior poses, twists)
    • Long walk or hike
    • Foam rolling + mobility exercises

Sunday – Rest & Recovery

  • Take a full rest day OR engage in light movement:
    • Light stretching or yoga (15–30 minutes)
    • Short walk in nature or around your area
    • Hydrate well and focus on nutritious meals
    • Optional: Practice mindfulness or meditation (5–10 minutes)
