Monday – Full Body Strength

  • Warm-up (5–10 min): Arm swings, light jog
  • Strength Focus:
    • Barbell Squats – 4 sets of 8–10 reps
    • Deadlifts – 3 sets of 6 reps
    • Pull-Ups – 3 sets to failure
    • Bench Press – 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Cool down: Full-body stretch

Tuesday – Upper Body Hypertrophy

  • Warm-up: Jump rope, dynamic stretches
  • Workout:
    • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 4 sets of 10 reps
    • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Barbell Rows – 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Triceps Dips – 3 sets to failure
  • Cool down: Shoulder and arm stretches

Wednesday – Lower Body Strength

  • Warm-up: Light cycling or bodyweight squats
  • Workout:
    • Front Squats – 4 sets of 6 reps
    • Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets of 8 reps
    • Leg Press – 4 sets of 10 reps
    • Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Cool down: Hamstring and quad stretches

Thursday – Active Recovery

  • Light cardio: Brisk walking or cycling (20–30 min)
  • Mobility Work:
    • Foam rolling
    • Yoga or light stretching session

Friday – Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Warm-up: Dynamic arm swings and light bench
  • Workout:
    • Flat Bench Press – 4 sets of 8 reps
    • Overhead Shoulder Press – 4 sets of 10 reps
    • Incline Dumbbell Fly – 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Triceps Rope Pushdown – 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Cool down: Arm and chest stretches

Saturday – Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

  • Warm-up: Jump rope, dynamic upper body stretches
  • Workout:
    • Deadlifts – 4 sets of 6 reps
    • Lat Pulldowns – 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Dumbbell Curls – 4 sets of 12 reps
    • Face Pulls – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Cool down: Back and bicep stretches

Sunday – Rest Day

  • No structured workout
  • Focus on nutrition, hydration, and sleep
  • Optional: Light stretching or a walk
